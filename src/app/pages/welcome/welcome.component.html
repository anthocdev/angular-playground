Introduction

Hi, a list of small projects built on top of the Angular front-end will be available on this subdomain. While there isn't much to see here yet, a few apps are in the process of 'polishing' as you read this.

I plan to utilize this site for feedback and easy access to any Angular modules I work on. There are also plans for other framework subdomains, which will happen depending on the performance of this one.

Mobile compatibility is currently minimal to non-existent. Adaptations will be made in the future.
